Beware the creatures that lurk in the dark of night.

Fight off hoards of monsters as the janitor in this arcade beat-em-up.

This game was made by Adam Morales(Programmer) and Nico Papalia(Artist)

Controls are as follow:

Z - Light attack

X - Heavy attack

Space - Jump

Escape - Pause

Enter - Press enter when you don't know what else to press
