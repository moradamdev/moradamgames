An adventure puzzle game for PC.

Ochen is about a young kid who, after being thrown out of the orphanage, finds a strange nation where everyone is cursed with masks. By solving puzzles in the dungeons and defeating the dungeon bosses you learn more about the curse and the nation you've stumbled into.

This game was made entirely by me, Adam Morales. It is my first game and was made in exactly one year as a challenge to myself to see what I could do in a limited amount of time. Hope you enjoy it!

Things to note:

*Most computers should be able to run this game as it is not graphically intense

**Game is pretty loud on startup

***Nerfed the Ice boss because people said he was too hard to beat. Lowered his health from 20 to 15
